Wishes.inc

A place where giving happens openly between people.

At Wishes Inc., every donation earns you cashback, instantly added to your virtual donation wallet linked with Apple or Google Wallet. You can use these rewards to support more charities or enjoy savings while shopping at over 80,000 partnered retailers. It’s a seamless way to give back and get rewarded—turning your generosity into ongoing impact and everyday benefits. With Wishes, your donations work harder for you and the causes you care about.

Cashback Rewards

Every donation you make with Wishes Inc. nets you cashback, which goes towards your virtual donation wallet. You can then use your rewards to give further to charity or splurge on them as savings from your purchases.

Exclusive Savings and Discounts

Get access to an expanding group of 80,000+ stores offering exclusive discounts, offers, and savings to Wishes Inc. users. It's our token of appreciation for your kindness.

Complete Transparency

Understand precisely where your contribution is being directed and the way it's affecting the issues that matter to you. Our site offers complete transparency so that you can track the immediate effects of your donation.
