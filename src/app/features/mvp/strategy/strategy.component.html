AI Scaling Strategy

Transforming charitable giving through intelligent, personalized experiences

Wishes.inc is an innovative donation platform that transforms charitable giving through its virtual credit card system, transparency mechanisms, and rewards program. The platform turns donations into virtual credit cards that are pushed to Apple and Google wallets, providing donors with instant access and full transparency on how their donations are spent.

Founded by Chase Harmer, a payments entrepreneur with 26+ years of experience who was one of the pioneers of virtual credit card technology, Wishes.inc operates as a 501(c)(3) public charity with a mission to certify the impact of donations for nonprofits and donors, help causes fundraise transparently, and impact 1 billion people over the next 10 years.

Virtual Credit Card Donation System

Converts donations into virtual credit cards integrated with Apple and Google wallets

Complete Transparency

Rewards System
